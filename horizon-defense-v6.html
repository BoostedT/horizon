// Organization Dossier

FORGED IN THE BLACK

Horizon Defense was founded on a single doctrine: no contract is too dangerous, no supply line too complex. Born from veterans of the Vanduul Contested Zones, we combined frontline combat expertise with deep logistics engineering to offer what no other org could — total operational capability.

We operate across the Stanton and Pyro systems, providing armed escort, hostile zone extraction, fleet logistics, and rapid response to allied organizations and independent contractors alike. Our engineers keep the fleet running; our operators keep the crew alive.

Horizon Defense is not just a military outfit. We are a self-sustaining force projection asset — capable of projecting power anywhere in the 'verse within hours of activation.

  • Disciplined operations with clear chain of command
  • Engineering & logistics backbone for every engagement
  • Cross-system rapid deployment capability
  • Strict ROE — we take contracts, not war crimes
